Summary

Hideo Kojima is video game director, write, producer, and designer.He was
hired by Konami in 1986 to work on a new game project known as Metal Gear.
He is best known for creating stealth genre game series known as Metal Gear
Solid.In 2015, Kojima left Konami to start his own game company Kojima
Productions.SONY entertainment and Guerilla games supported his decision,
and Guerilla games gifted their game engine with source code to Kojima
Productions.He released a new genre of strand game known as
Death Stranding in November 2019.
